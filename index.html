The Major Memory App

(Graphic Designer Wanted)

Introduction

Good day. My name is Ryan Els and I am a programmer from South Africa.

I am looking for a graphic designer who would like to collaborate with me on this project.

The purpose of this project is first and foremost a chance for us to show off our skills and to create a product we can both be proud of.

The mission is to eventually publish the app to all available app stores and to monetize the app. Until that point this project will be a labour of love.

Once the app is published you will be co-owner of the app and share in any profits up to 49%.



If you are interested please read on...

The App

The app is a memory training app based on the Major Memory System.

The app will make use of a gaming concept to keep the users attention and make the learning experience fun and interactive.

There are many memory training apps on the market. And some of them are doing very well with over 10 million downloads.

So there is definitely a market for this type of app.

What will set this app apart from the competition is the fun and entertaining approach we will bring to the app.

As yet the app does not have an official name. But the working title is the Major Memory App. We can brainstorm to come up with something a little more catchy.

Want to join the project?

I believe in raw talent and I believe there are some excellent graphic designers out there that have never had a chance to prove themselves yet. So I want to give everybody a chance to apply regardless of who they are or where they are from. What I am looking for is real talent. Somebody with a flare for design and a sense of pride in their work. Because I want the app to be of high quality and for it to stand out and have that WOW factor.

So if you are interested in collaborating with me I require one thing from you. You must pass the test below.

The Test

I want you to design the Major for the app. Nothing too fancy. You can even do a couple of pencil sketches for now. I just need to see the quality of your design work. The Major is the central character for the app. He is a Major in the army.

Good luck with your application.
